Get in Touch

Thank you for using TVSphere.
We value your feedback, suggestions, and questions. If you need assistance, want to report an issue, or have general inquiries, we're here to help.

Customer Support

If you're experiencing problems while using TVSphere, please contact us with as much detail as possible, including:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • TVSphere version
  • Description of the issue
  • Screenshots (if available)

This helps us investigate and resolve issues more quickly.

Business Inquiries

For business partnerships, collaborations, media requests, or other professional inquiries, please contact us using the email address below.

Copyright Concerns

TVSphere does not host or own any streaming content. If you believe that any publicly available stream displayed in the application infringes your rights, please contact us with the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your contact information
  • The name of the TV channel or radio station
  • A description of your concern

We will review your request and respond as appropriate.

Before Contacting Us

Please check that:

  • Your internet connection is working properly.
  • You are using the latest version of TVSphere.
  • The issue is not caused by the original streaming provider.
