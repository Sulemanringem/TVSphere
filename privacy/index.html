Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

Welcome to TVSphere

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how TVSphere collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application and website.

About TVSphere

TVSphere is a platform that helps users discover publicly available television channels and FM radio stations from around the world.
TVSphere does not host, upload, broadcast, modify, or control any television or radio streams. All streaming content is provided by third-party sources and remains the property of its respective owners.

Information We Collect

TVSphere does not require you to create an account before using the app.
Depending on the version of the application, we may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Device language
  • Approximate country or region
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information

This information helps us improve the reliability, performance, and user experience of TVSphere.

Location

TVSphere may determine your approximate country based on your IP address, device language, or time zone to provide localized content.
We do not collect or store your precise GPS location.

Local Storage

To improve your experience, TVSphere may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Favorite channels
  • Recently viewed channels
  • Theme preference
  • Selected language
  • Preferred country
  • Other application settings

This information remains on your device unless you choose to remove it.

Third-Party Services

TVSphere may use third-party services to provide certain features, including analytics, crash reporting, and advertising in future versions.
Each third-party service has its own privacy policy and data practices.

Advertising

The current version of TVSphere does not display advertisements.
Future versions may include advertising services. If advertisements are introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by TVSphere. However, no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

TVSphere is not specifically intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective once published on our website or within the application.
