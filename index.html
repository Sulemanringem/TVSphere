Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

About TVSphere

TVSphere is a global TV and radio discovery application that helps users explore publicly available television channels and radio stations from around the world.

TVSphere does not host, own, upload, modify, or control any streaming content. All streams belong to their respective owners.

Information We Collect

TVSphere does not require user registration.

  • No username required
  • No password required
  • No personal profile required

We may collect limited technical information including device language, Android version, app version, country, and crash reports to improve the application.

Location

TVSphere may automatically determine your approximate country using your device language, timezone, or IP address in order to display popular channels relevant to your region.

Your precise GPS location is never collected.

Favorites & Settings

Favorites, recent history, theme, language, and country preferences are stored locally on your device.

Streaming Content

TVSphere only indexes publicly available streams. We do not host, own, broadcast, or modify any television or radio content.

Advertising

The current version of TVSphere does not display advertisements. Future versions may include advertising services such as Google AdMob. This Privacy Policy will be updated before advertisements are introduced.

Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.
